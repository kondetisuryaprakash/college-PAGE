About the Department

The ECE Department provides a rigorous academic foundation and real-world problem-solving skills. Our curriculum spans signal processing, embedded systems, IoT, VLSI, and communication technologies.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a premier center of excellence producing globally competent ECE professionals.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality technical education.
  • Promote research and innovation in ECE.
  • Strengthen industry-academia collaboration.
  • Encourage entrepreneurship and leadership.
